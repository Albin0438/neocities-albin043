When Custom ROM Installation Gone Wrong.

Today i decided to flash an another custom rom into my redmi note 8. But actually, I were already in a custom rom called InfinityX.

InfinityX is an amazing custom rom project based on LineageOS. But the reason i decided to replace it is i downloaded and flashed the Gapps version, where google apps are bundled in as a stock rom. But eventually i find out that even though am in a custom rom google still know too much about me. Too many background processes runs in the background and most of them are just google servers. Panic mode activated and am started about worrying about privacy, so i decided to install LineageOS which is by default decently degoogled and faster, still updated android 16 based solid choice. But still there are some harsh realities like banking apps wont work, many games require to install their app from official play store when i prefer aurora store in LineageOS, device safetynet integrity will fail, google apps won't work, etc. But actually that's okay. I don't care.

So i went to LineageOS official website but downloaded the wrong zip file. I downloaded Xiaomi Redmi Note 5A Lite custom rom zip file instead of Xiaomi Redmi Note 8 zip. Am not blind, but i were less careful in belief that i already done it too many times it will won't end up in an issue. But my device went into a bootloop, that i am unable to install any custom recovery via fastboot.

Another fact is that, i faced this same issue months ago, instead of just asking a cloud based ai, i never tried to write it down in a notebook, or never tried to learn the command. Just looked at ai, fixed, move on. But that was a mistake, i must written that into a notebook.

I only owned 1 mobile device. Mobile data via that device was my only way to access internet, so after this happened, until my mother return to home, i was helpless because i needed internet to even download another recovery, or even just search on google. Atleast depending on a local AI would be smarter at this point.

But all of this was not that bad at all, today i saw the outside world, i touched the grass in my garden which i were unaware until today my phone destroyed. I was always searching for a reason to put phone down, i needed to reduce my screen time so i downloaded many screen time apps which end up in wasting data and deleted them without a second thought. Actually, i required to entirely put down the phone, as the problem is the device, so no app or program inside it can fix it. my answer was this much close which i didn't saw until now. Thankfully, i downloaded some animes on my lap, so i watched it after around 3 months short form content consumption and that was awesome. I really loved those animes and it's story.

Whatever happened today ended up in a brighter way. it opened my eyes. Now my fear is, when i fixed my phone won't i return to those habits again and the good things will end? I know my parents, house, pets, household, neighbourhood, age and freedom aren't gonna stay long. They will all disappear eventually and i will hit more harsh ralities. But still why our mind prefer this mobile over everything else? isn't that really disturbing in some way? Or am i just a jerk?
