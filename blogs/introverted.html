The Blessing of Being an Introvert

Hi guys! I am Albin, and this is my first blog post. As an introvert and someone who is very sensitive, I often struggle to get through each day. My mind is always filled with overthinking and regret about not being an extrovert.

I know both introverts and extroverts have their own advantages and disadvantages. Many successful persons in this world are were introverts. They become successful not because they successfully converted them to an extrovert, but understanding their inner deep self and spending time with themselves when they feel alone in a distracted world.

So, just like them, we are all able to build our own path and a successful career by just spending time with ourself and fixing our problems created by an unhealthy environment around us.

This is a post am just writing experssing my thoughts and also a letter for all introverted techies who think a successful life for them in a distracted world is near impossible. That's just a stae of mind formed by what we saw until today and understanded the world. But the reality is there is more things to experience and see than we just created in our mind.

Just don't lost hope and never give up.
