Uses

I often get questions on my videos about my desktop and the software I use. Most of the time, it’s something like: “How do I make my desktop look cool like yours?” This page lists everything I use on a daily basis.

My goal with the software and operating system I use is to be as efficient as possible— to use my computer almost “at the speed of thought.” Minimal software helps me achieve that, although it may not be for everyone.

Some links here may be affiliate links to services and products I use. I only recommend things I personally use and trust.
